The network for Open Hardware in German-speaking regions

Our goals

  • We are a network of open hardware experts, institutional representatives, and policy makers working to promote open hardware.
  • The focus is on developing strategies and recommendations for action that show how the potential of open hardware can be fully unleashed.
  • In doing so, we combine economic, scientific and civil society interests to promote the best possible and sustainable anchoring of open hardware.
